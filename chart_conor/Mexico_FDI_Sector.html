Mexico's FDI Broken Down by Sector (2012)

The graphic points to a few interesting trends:

  • There is a notable absence of investments in energy. This is the year before energy markets were privatised and opened up to foreign investors and the last year of available data on FDI.
  • While manufacturing continues to serve as the largest sector receiving FDI, other sectors are growing.


  • By pursuing privitization reforms, Mexico expanded which sectors received FDI inflows.
As FDI grew in Mexico, so did
the variety of sectors that received investment.
